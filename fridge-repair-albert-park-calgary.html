Refrigerator Repair Services in Albert Park

Albert Park's heritage of working-class home ownership means our technicians encounter an unusual range of refrigerator vintages on any given day in this inner-SE neighbourhood. The neighbourhood's 1940s and 1950s bungalows often house older Kenmore and GE top-freezer models — solid, mechanically simple machines that reached 25-35 years of service before needing attention. These older units commonly develop defrost system failures: the defrost heater or defrost thermostat fails, ice accumulates on the evaporator coils, and the fridge gradually loses cooling capacity while the freezer continues to function. Our technicians recognize this pattern immediately and replace the defrost components on the spot, typically for $140–$200 in parts and labour.

Albert Park also has a significant number of newer Samsung and LG refrigerators installed in renovated properties and rental units. Samsung French door models from 2012–2020 are among our most frequent service calls across all SE Calgary neighbourhoods — the ice maker assembly in these units is prone to icing up due to a design issue with the water supply tube routing, causing the ice maker to stop producing and water to pool at the bottom of the fridge compartment. We carry the corrected tube clips and updated ice maker components to perform the standard fix. LG refrigerators with linear compressors manufactured between 2014 and 2018 may still be within the compressor's 10-year warranty period — we always check eligibility before recommending a compressor replacement.

For rental properties in Albert Park, a non-functioning fridge is an urgent matter requiring same-day response. We prioritize fridge calls accordingly and carry the most common parts for the most common Albert Park brands at the vehicle level.

Frequently Asked Questions — Albert Park Fridge Repair

My GE fridge is 25 years old and not cooling — should I repair or replace?

If the repair involves the defrost system (heater, thermostat, or defrost board), the cost is typically $140–$220 and the machine will last many more years. If the sealed refrigerant system or compressor has failed on a 25-year-old GE, replacement is usually the better value. We'll give you an honest comparison before any work begins.

How do you fix a Samsung ice maker that keeps freezing up?

The root cause is a poorly routed water supply tube that freezes and blocks water flow. The permanent fix involves installing corrected tube routing clips and, in many cases, replacing the ice maker module with an updated version. We carry these parts in stock. The repair typically takes 1–1.5 hours.

Is my LG compressor still under warranty?

LG's linear compressor warranty covers 10 years from date of purchase for models manufactured between 2014 and 2022. If your LG fridge was purchased within that window, we check the serial number and manufacture date before recommending any compressor work — warranty repairs are processed through LG's authorized service program at no parts cost to you.

Can you service fridges in rental units and coordinate with tenants?

Yes. We work with Albert Park property managers and landlords regularly. We can contact tenants directly to arrange access, complete the repair, and send documentation to the landlord. Fridge calls in rental units are given same-day priority when possible to minimize food loss.
