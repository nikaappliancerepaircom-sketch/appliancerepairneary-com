Won't Agitate or Spin

Motor couplers, agitator dogs, and worn drive belts are the most common cause in older Frigidaire and GE top-loaders. Straightforward replacement in one visit.

Won't Drain

Drain pump blockages and pump motor failures affect all brands. Samsung and LG drain pump assemblies stocked in our service vehicle for same-day repair.

Door Gasket Leak

Front-load washer door gaskets (boot seals) tear with normal use. We carry Samsung WF and LG WM series gaskets for immediate replacement.

Excessive Noise / Vibration

Worn drum bearings and failed shock absorbers cause excessive movement and banging. We diagnose and replace worn components to restore quiet operation.

Lid Switch / Won't Start

Failed lid switches on top-loaders and door latch failures on front-loaders prevent the cycle from beginning. Common on machines over 15 years old.

Control Board Failure

Failed control boards on modern front-loaders show as error codes or no response from the control panel. Diagnosed and replaced with OEM-compatible boards.

Frequently Asked Questions — Forest Heights Washer Repair

Is it worth repairing a Frigidaire top-loader that's 25+ years old?

Yes, in many cases. Older Frigidaire top-loaders are mechanically simple and parts-rich. A motor coupler replacement ($110–$190) or lid switch fix ($100–$170) can restore years of additional service from a machine that's otherwise in good condition. We'll inspect the drum, tub, and overall condition before recommending repair versus replacement.

My Samsung front-load washer is leaving a mouldy smell — is this a repair issue?

A mouldy smell is usually a maintenance issue rather than a repair — mould accumulates in the door gasket folds and the drum when the door is kept closed after cycles. However, if the gasket has physical tears or the drainage isn't clearing fully after each cycle, that accelerates mould growth and a repair may be needed. We check both during a service call.

My LG front-loader is making a loud grinding noise during spin — what's causing it?

Loud grinding during spin is almost always drum bearing failure. In LG front-loaders, the rear drum bearing and outer drum are often replaced together. This is a 2–3 hour repair that significantly extends the machine's service life. We'll confirm the diagnosis and give you a full quote before proceeding.
