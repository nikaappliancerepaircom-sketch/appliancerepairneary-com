Washer Problems We Repair Near You in Willowdale

From simple fixes to complex repairs, we handle every issue below with same-day parts and expert diagnosis.

Won't Start or Power On

We check the power board, lid switch, thermal fuse, and control module. Most power issues are resolved in under an hour.

Water Leaking on Floor

Leaks from door gaskets, pump seals, inlet hoses, or tub-to-pump connections. We locate the source and seal it same day.

Loud Banging or Grinding

Worn drum bearings, failing shock absorbers, or loose counterweights. Continuing to run risks permanent tub damage.

Unbalanced or Excessive Vibration
