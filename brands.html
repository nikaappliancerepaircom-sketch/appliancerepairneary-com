Why Brand Expertise Matters for Appliance Repair

Every appliance manufacturer uses its own proprietary diagnostic codes, motor configurations, control boards, and seal types. A technician who has only ever worked on Whirlpool units may diagnose a Samsung inverter compressor issue incorrectly, or replace a part that is not actually the root cause of the failure. That is why Appliance Repair Neary invests in ongoing brand-specific training for all technicians serving Toronto and the GTA.

Samsung and LG smart appliances communicate fault codes via Wi-Fi diagnostic apps — our technicians use the manufacturer tools, not generic readers. Bosch and Miele European-engineered units require specific calibration procedures after board replacements. Whirlpool and Maytag (both owned by Whirlpool Corp) share many parts, but GE Profile and GE Cafe lines use entirely different control architectures. Knowing these details means a faster, more accurate repair for you.

We stock OEM and OEM-equivalent parts for all the brands listed above, which means typical repairs cost $120–$400 and are completed on the first visit. No waiting a week for parts to be ordered in from a distributor. All work carries a 90-day parts and labour warranty.

Brand Repair — Common Questions

Answers about our brand coverage and how we handle different appliance makes.

Do you carry OEM parts for Samsung and LG appliances?

Yes. We stock OEM and OEM-equivalent parts for Samsung, LG, and all major brands. Most parts needed for common faults are carried on the technician's van, allowing same-visit repairs without waiting for special orders.

Can you repair high-end brands like Bosch, Miele, and KitchenAid?

Yes. Our technicians are trained on European-engineered brands including Bosch and Miele as well as premium North American lines like KitchenAid. These brands require specific calibration and proprietary diagnostic procedures which our team is equipped to handle.

What is the typical cost to repair a brand-name appliance?

Repair costs typically range $120–$400 regardless of brand. Premium brands like Bosch or Miele may have higher part costs but labour is the same rate. We provide a written upfront quote before any work begins.

My appliance brand is not listed — can you still repair it?

Very likely yes. We service most major and many mid-tier brands. Call (437) 524-1053 and tell us the brand and model — we will confirm right away whether we can help and quote a diagnostic visit.
