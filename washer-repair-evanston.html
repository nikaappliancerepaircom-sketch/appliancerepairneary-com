Washer Repair in Evanston — Fast, Reliable, Local

Evanston is a large, densely built northern Calgary suburb with a high proportion of young families who bought into new construction during the 2000s and 2010s. Builder-grade Samsung and LG front-load washer pairs from this era are now 8–15 years old and presenting with post-warranty wear failures at high volume. We reach Evanston in approximately 35 minutes via Stoney Trail and route north-end service calls efficiently to minimize travel time. The volume of similar-vintage washers in Evanston creates a predictable service pattern: Samsung front-loaders from the 2010s are developing drum spider fractures — the cast aluminium spider arm that connects the drum to the bearing shaft cracks from metal fatigue after 10–12 years of high-load family use. This is a more labour-intensive repair but typically still cost-effective compared to replacement when the machine is otherwise in good condition. LG front-loaders in Evanston are primarily presenting with the OE drain error from pump filter accumulation — especially common in families washing heavily soiled work and sports clothing. We carry LG and Samsung pump filters, door boots, shock absorbers, and drum bearing kits on every Evanston run. Whirlpool top-loaders from the same era frequently have agitator coupling failures and lid switch issues that are relatively quick repairs. For all brands, we assess whether the repair cost is proportionate to the remaining value of the unit and provide an honest recommendation when the numbers do not favour repair.
