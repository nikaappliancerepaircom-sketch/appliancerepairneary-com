Common Washer Problems in Collingwood

  • GE top-load agitator coupling failure — the plastic motor coupler connecting the transmission to the agitator motor wears and splits, stopping both agitation and spin while leaving clothes wet
  • GE temperature selector switch failure — the rotary temperature selector loses contact on one or more settings, limiting wash temperature options or causing constant cold-water filling
  • Kenmore top-load lid switch wear — the lid switch microswitch contact wears after thousands of cycles, causing the spin cycle to disengage or fail to activate
  • Whirlpool HE inlet valve screen clog — the fine mesh screen inside the water inlet valve traps sediment from Collingwood's older supply lines, restricting fill flow and triggering long fill times or error codes
  • LG front-load tub bearing failure — tub bearings in LG front-loaders over 10 years old develop a loud rumbling noise during spin that escalates progressively if not addressed
  • Samsung drain pump impeller chip — a small object (button, coin) chips the polypropylene impeller in Samsung drain pumps, causing incomplete drainage and a 5E/nE error code

Frequently Asked Questions

My GE top-loader stopped both agitating and spinning at the same time — what failed? +
When both agitation and spin stop simultaneously on a GE top-loader, the motor coupler is almost always the cause. The coupler is a three-piece rubber and plastic assembly that connects the motor to the transmission. When it fails, both functions stop. Replacement takes about 45 minutes and costs $85–$145.
Can you fix an LG front-loader with a loud rumbling noise during spin? +
Yes. Rumbling during spin on an LG front-loader is a tub bearing problem. We replace the bearing before it progresses to drum shaft damage, which would significantly increase repair cost. LG tub bearing replacement is a 2–3 hour repair that restores quiet operation and prevents secondary damage.
My Whirlpool washer takes very long to fill — is that a water pressure problem? +
Long fill times on Whirlpool HE washers are usually caused by a partially clogged inlet valve screen rather than house water pressure. The fine mesh screen inside the valve captures sediment from the supply line. We clear or replace the screen and confirm fill flow rate before completing the call.

Washer Care Guide for Collingwood Calgary Homeowners

A well-maintained washer lasts 12–15 years. One that is not typically fails at 7–9 years. Calgary's hard water (150–200 ppm from the Bow River) and temperature cycling create extra wear — here is how Collingwood Calgary homeowners can extend their washer's life based on the repairs our technicians see most often:

  • Monthly: Run a hot maintenance cycle with a washer cleaner tablet (Affresh or equivalent). This removes detergent residue and mineral deposits that accumulate in the drum and pump filter from Calgary's hard water supply.
  • Monthly: Leave the front-loader door ajar between cycles to prevent mould growth in the drum. Wipe the door boot gasket with a damp cloth — this is where most front-loader odours and seal deterioration begin in Collingwood Calgary homes.
  • Quarterly: Check hose connections at the back of the unit. Calgary's Chinook temperature swings can stress hose connections — slow leaks at the connection point often go undetected until they cause floor damage.
  • Always: Use HE detergent in HE washers. Standard detergent over-suds in low-water machines and leaves residue that accelerates drum bearing wear — one of the most common causes of the grinding noise we repair in Collingwood Calgary homes.

Common Washer Problems We Repair in Collingwood Calgary

Washing machines in Collingwood Calgary face a combination of regular wear and Calgary-specific challenges — hard water mineral buildup from the Bow River supply at 150–200 ppm, and Calgary's Chinook temperature swings that stress seals and hoses throughout the year. These are the washer faults our technicians see most often:

  • →Drum not spinning — a failed lid switch (top-loaders) or door latch (front-loaders) prevents the spin cycle from engaging. Common on HE washers over 5 years old in Collingwood Calgary homes.

  • →F21, E3, or UL error codes — these indicate specific component failures. We diagnose and resolve with a definitive repair, not a reset workaround.
