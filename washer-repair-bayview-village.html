Common Issues We Fix

Washer Problems We Repair Near You in Bayview Village

From simple fixes to complex repairs, we handle every issue below with same-day parts and expert diagnosis.

🔴
Grinding Noise Every Cycle

Drum bearing failure is progressive — early diagnosis avoids cracking the outer tub. We carry bearing kits for all major brands.

💦
Slow Leak Behind Machine

Hidden leaks from supply hose connections, fill valve weeping, or a cracked inlet manifold. We pressure-test every connection.

🔍
Starts Then Immediately Stops

Door interlock failure, motor relay dropping out, or control board intermittent fault. Systematic electrical testing identifies the cause.

🌡
Water Won't Heat Up

The heating element, thermistor, or inlet valve hot-side solenoid has failed. Cold-only washes leave detergent residue and reduce cleaning.

⚡
Repeated UE or OE Codes

UE means unbalanced load; OE means drain obstruction. Recurring codes after reset indicate hardware failure requiring professional repair.

🚫
Detergent Drawer Overflows

Clogged dispenser channels, excessive detergent use, or low water pressure restricting the rinse flow through the dispenser housing.
