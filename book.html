Why Book Same-Day Appliance Repair with Us?

When a household appliance breaks down — a fridge that stops cooling, a washer that floods the laundry room, or a dryer that simply refuses to heat — you need fast, reliable help. Appliance Repair Neary dispatches certified local technicians from the closest available position to your Toronto or GTA address. That means shorter wait times and a better chance of a same-day fix.

Booking FAQ

How fast can a technician arrive after booking?

For same-day bookings made before noon, a technician typically arrives within 1–3 hours. We dispatch the nearest available certified tech to your address. You will receive a confirmation with your technician's ETA.

What happens if you cannot fix my appliance on the first visit?

In the rare case a specialist part is not on the van, we order it immediately and return for a second visit at no additional call-out charge. Most repairs are completed on the first visit because we pre-stock parts for all major brands.

How much does same-day appliance repair cost?

Typical repair costs range from $120–$400 depending on the appliance and issue. The diagnostic fee ($0–$85) is waived when you proceed with the repair. We provide a written quote before starting any work — you approve the price first.

Do weekend and evening bookings cost extra?

No. We charge the same standard rates on evenings, Saturdays, and Sundays. Our hours are Monday–Saturday 8am–8pm and Sunday 9am–6pm — all at the same price as weekday daytime appointments.
