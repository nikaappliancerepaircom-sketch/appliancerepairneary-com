Common Dishwasher Repair Issues in Garrison Green

  • Bosch circulation pump failure — dishes not washing, humming sound, common in 10–18 year old Bosch units
  • Calcium scale buildup — Calgary's 150–200 ppm water blocks spray arm nozzles and inlet valves
  • Samsung control board fault — dishwasher won't start or stops mid-cycle, error codes on display
  • Door gasket leak — water pooling on kitchen floor, especially in compact townhome kitchens
  • Drain pump blockage — standing water at cycle end, food debris or scale restricting pump impeller
  • LG touchpad failure — unresponsive buttons, cycle selection not registering
