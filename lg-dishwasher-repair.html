Our technicians have extensive experience with LG dishwasher repair faults. We diagnose accurately and repair on the first visit in most cases.

Washer OE Error (Drain)

LG's OE error code means the washer is not draining. A clogged pump filter, failed drain pump, or kinked drain hose causes this. The pump filter in LG washers needs monthly cleaning — we fix the immediate fault and show you how to maintain it.

Dishwasher OE / AE Error

LG dishwashers display OE (drain error) or AE (water leak) codes pointing to pump, drain hose, or door seal failures. We diagnose the specific fault and repair it without guesswork.
