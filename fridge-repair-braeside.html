Braeside's position along Fish Creek Provincial Park gives it one of the most desirable settings in SW Calgary — a quiet neighbourhood where long-term families have put down roots, updated their homes thoughtfully, and generally maintained their appliances well. The refrigerators in Braeside kitchens span generations: some homes still run the original Frigidaire or GE top-freezer from the 1990s that has simply been reliable; others have mid-era Whirlpool or Maytag side-by-sides from the 2000s; and kitchens renovated in the past decade typically have an LG French door or Samsung Family Hub model. We service all of these, reaching Braeside in about 24 minutes from our SW Calgary dispatch location.

The most frequent refrigerator call we receive from Braeside is a warm fridge with a cold freezer — the classic defrost system failure pattern. In a 15–20 year old Whirlpool or GE, the defrost heater element burns out and the evaporator coil gradually ices over. The freezer maintains temperature because it's directly adjacent to the coil, but the fresh food compartment warms as the blocked fan can no longer circulate cold air. Replacing the defrost heater, thermal limiter, and defrost thermostat costs $140–$240 and is a same-day repair that restores full function. For the LG and Samsung generation, the most common fault is ice maker module failure — Calgary's 150–200 ppm hard water eventually deposits sediment in the inlet valve filter screen, blocking water flow to the ice maker assembly.

Nature-Adjacent Living and Your Refrigerator

Braeside families who back onto Fish Creek trails often have additional refrigeration in the garage — a secondary fridge or chest freezer for bulk storage, game meat from hunting trips, or summer produce from local markets. Calgary's climate creates specific challenges for garage fridges: in winter, when ambient temperatures drop below 10°C, most residential refrigerators stop cooling because the thermostat reads the ambient temperature as already "cold enough" and the compressor doesn't start. This is a design issue rather than a mechanical fault. A garage kit resolves it by adding a heater strip near the thermostat. We install these and advise on the right solution for your specific model.

Repair vs. Replace

Braeside residents tend toward considered decisions rather than impulse replacements. An LG French door at 12 years, priced at $1,500 new, justifies a $200–$320 ice maker or fan replacement. We give you the numbers before starting any work.

Common Faults
Refrigerator Problems We Fix in Braeside
Fridge Not Cooling
Defrost system failure causing evaporator ice-over and airflow blockage. Fridge warm, freezer cold. Very common in 12–20 year old units. Same-day repair in most cases.
Ice Maker Stopped
Ice maker module failure or inlet valve blockage from Calgary hard water sediment. Common on LG, Samsung, and Whirlpool French door models.
Frost in Freezer
Failed defrost heater, thermostat, or timer — frost coats the evaporator coil. Defrost system replacement restores normal frost-free operation.
Garage Fridge Not Cooling
Calgary winter ambient temperature drops below 10°C — compressor doesn't trigger. A garage kit resolves this without mechanical repair to the fridge.
Water Pooling Inside
Blocked defrost drain — meltwater can't drain and pools under the crispers. Quick fix that prevents floor damage and mould growth under the fridge.
Compressor Clicking / Not Running
Failed start relay or overload protector — a $60–$90 part that often prevents compressor replacement. We test before recommending compressor work.

How quickly can you come to Braeside? +
About 24 minutes from our SW Calgary base — our most southerly SW neighbourhood. For a warm fridge we prioritize same-day service. Book before noon for an afternoon slot. For urgent situations involving spoiling food, call and we'll do our best to fit you in same-day.
My fridge is warm but the freezer is still cold — is this a serious problem? +
It's a common and fully repairable problem — defrost system failure. The evaporator coil ices over and blocks the fan that circulates cold air to the fridge section. Replacing the defrost heater, thermal limiter, and defrost thermostat — $140–$240 — returns the fridge to full function in a single visit. Book quickly to protect your food.
My garage fridge doesn't cool in winter — what's happening? +
A very common Calgary issue. When garage ambient temperatures drop below about 10°C, the refrigerator's thermostat doesn't call for the compressor to run because it reads the ambient as already cold. The fridge stops cooling even though it's plugged in. This is solved by a garage kit — a heater strip that keeps the thermostat area warm enough to trigger the compressor. We install garage kits for most major brands.
Is a 20-year-old Frigidaire refrigerator worth repairing? +
For a single fault on a mechanically sound fridge, usually yes. Frigidaire and GE top-freezer models from the early 2000s were built with simple, reliable components. Defrost heater and door gasket repairs at $130–$240 are excellent value. We'll give you an honest assessment on-site — if the compressor is failing or you're facing multiple simultaneous issues, we'll say replacement is the smarter move.
