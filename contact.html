FAQ

Contact & Booking Questions

Common questions about how to reach us and what to expect when you call.

How do I book same-day appliance repair?

Call (437) 524-1053 or use the online booking form on this page. For same-day service, call before noon on any weekday or Saturday. A local technician near your address will be dispatched with a 2-hour arrival window.

Do you charge extra for evening or weekend calls?

No. We charge the same standard rates regardless of the time or day. Evenings, Saturdays, and Sundays are all at standard pricing. We are open Monday–Saturday 8am–8pm and Sunday 9am–6pm.

How quickly will a technician arrive at my address?

In most GTA areas, same-day bookings result in a technician arriving within 1–4 hours. We dispatch the nearest available certified technician to your specific address, not just the first available tech in the city.

What information should I have ready when I call?

Have your full address, the appliance brand and model number (usually on a sticker inside the door or on the back), and a description of the issue ready. This helps us dispatch the right technician with the most likely parts needed for your repair.
