Calgary Winter and Your Refrigerator

Calgary winters introduce a specific refrigeration challenge: garage fridges or wine coolers in unheated spaces can stop cooling when ambient temperatures drop below 10°C, because the compressor relies on a minimum ambient temperature to maintain head pressure. Conversely, high summer ambient in a poorly ventilated utility room stresses condenser coils. We diagnose both scenarios and advise on placement or insulation solutions that prevent recurrence — particularly relevant for Pump Hill homes with attached garages or wine cellars.

Common Faults
Refrigerator Problems We Fix
Not Cooling / Warm Fridge
Defrost system failure, condenser fan fault, or refrigerant issue. We diagnose and repair all cooling failures, including Sub-Zero sealed systems.
Ice Maker Not Working
Ice maker module, water inlet valve, or ice bucket auger failure. Common on LG, Samsung, Sub-Zero, and KitchenAid French door models.
Frost Buildup in Freezer
Failed defrost heater, thermal limiter, or defrost thermostat. The fridge warms while the freezer ices over — a classic defrost system fault.
Compressor Issues
Clicking compressor, start relay failure, or compressor overload. We test and replace start components before recommending compressor replacement.
Water Leaking / Pooling
Blocked defrost drain, cracked drain pan, or water filter housing leak. Quick fix that prevents floor damage and mould growth.
Door Seal / Gasket Worn
Worn door gaskets let warm air in, causing the compressor to overwork. New gaskets restore efficiency and are especially cost-effective on older units.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you repair Sub-Zero refrigerators? +
Yes. Sub-Zero repair is highly worthwhile given the replacement cost of $8,000–$14,000+. We service Sub-Zero ice maker assemblies, evaporator fans, defrost systems, compressor start components, and door gaskets. For sealed system work we assess on-site and advise on the repair path.
My fridge is warm but the freezer seems fine — what's wrong? +
This typically points to a defrost system fault — the evaporator coil ices over and blocks airflow to the fridge section. The freezer stays cold from direct contact, but the fridge warms. A defrost heater or thermostat replacement usually resolves this, and it's one of our most common repairs.

If you're seeing multiple simultaneous faults or compressor failure, we'll give you an honest assessment rather than push a repair that won't deliver value.
