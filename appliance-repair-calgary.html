Refrigerator and freezer repair Calgary

Fridge repairs account for roughly 35% of our Calgary call volume. Calgary's climate extremes — from −30°C winters to +35°C July afternoons — stress refrigeration systems more than in most Canadian cities. Common Calgary fridge failures: compressor cycling issues in attached garages during cold snaps, condenser coil icing from dry winter air, and ice maker scaling from Calgary's moderately hard tap water (City of Calgary water averages 165 mg/L total hardness). We service Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, Bosch, KitchenAid, Frigidaire, GE, Miele, Sub-Zero, and all other residential brands.

Washer and dryer repair Calgary

Laundry appliance repair is our second-highest volume category in Calgary. Top washer issues we handle: drum bearing failure in front-loaders (especially LG and Samsung after 5–7 years), door boot seal tears, control board failures, and drain pump blockages. For dryers, heating element failures dominate in electric models; gas dryer igniter issues are common in Calgary's natural-gas-heavy housing stock. Our technicians carry Universal heating elements and OEM igniters for rapid same-day resolution.

Dishwasher repair Calgary

Dishwasher repair volume peaks in Calgary neighbourhoods with older GE, Maytag, and Bosch units installed between 2005 and 2015. Calgary's moderately hard water accelerates spray arm clogging and float switch calcification. We also see high rates of dishwasher-not-draining calls across newer Marda Loop, Mission, and Bankview infill builds where drain line routing creates back-pressure. We address root cause, not just symptoms.

Oven, stove, and range repair Calgary

Calgary has one of Canada's highest rates of natural gas hookups — over 85% of single-family homes use gas for heating, and many kitchens run gas ranges. Our technicians are licensed to handle both gas and electric appliance repair. Common oven issues: bake element burnout in electric ranges, gas igniter cracks (fail-safe shuts off the burner), convection fan motor failures, and control board resets. We service slide-in, freestanding, and built-in ranges for all brands.
