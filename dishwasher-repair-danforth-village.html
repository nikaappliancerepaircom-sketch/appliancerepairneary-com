Common Issues We Fix

Dishwasher Problems We Repair Near You in Danforth Village

Same-day diagnosis and repair for the issues below. We arrive with parts ready to fix it right.

🔴
Loud Buzzing When Running

Wash motor startup capacitor failing, pump impeller hitting debris, or water inlet valve vibrating under pressure.

💦
Dripping from Under the Door

Lower door gasket compressed or torn, spray arm directing water at the seal gap, or overfill from a stuck inlet valve.

⚡
Display Shows Error — Won't Reset

Persistent errors after power cycling indicate hardware failure. Flood sensors, thermistors, and pump motors are common culprits.

🔍
Top Rack Items Still Dirty

Upper spray arm clogged with mineral deposits, low water pressure, or broken spray arm pivot seal reducing water flow.

🌡
Dishes Wet After Dry Cycle

Failed heating element, broken vent fan, or rinse aid depleted. Proper drying requires all three systems working together.

🚫
Won't Drain — Standing Water

Drain pump motor failure, blocked drain filter, or garbage disposal knockout plug not removed during installation.
