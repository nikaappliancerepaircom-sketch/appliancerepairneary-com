Refrigerator repair calls in Hamptons Calgary peak in summer when compressors work harder, and in winter when Calgary's dramatic Chinook temperature swings — from −20°C to +15°C in 24 hours — stress door seals, defrost systems, and compressor components.   • →Fridge not cooling or not cold enough — compressor, evaporator fan, or thermostat failure. The evaporator fan is the most common single-point failure on modern frost-free units in Hamptons Calgary.
