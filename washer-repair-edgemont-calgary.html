These are the washer faults our technicians see most often:

  • →Drum not spinning — a failed lid switch (top-loaders) or door latch (front-loaders) prevents the spin cycle from engaging. Common on HE washers over 5 years old in Edgemont Calgary homes.

Frequently Asked Questions — Edgemont Washer Repair

My Whirlpool washer in Edgemont makes noise but doesn't wash — what's wrong?+
The most likely cause is a failed motor coupler — the three-piece plastic coupler that connects the drive motor to the transmission. When it breaks, the motor runs but nothing moves. This is one of the most common repairs on Whirlpool and Kenmore direct-drive top-loaders built from the 1980s through the 2000s. The coupler is inexpensive and the repair is typically under $160 all-in.
What is the LG UE error code and what causes it?+
UE stands for unbalanced load. While it can be triggered by an actual uneven load, persistent UE errors during normal loads in older LG front-loaders almost always indicate drum bearing wear. The worn bearing allows the drum to wobble eccentrically at high speed, triggering the balance sensor. Drum bearing replacement resolves persistent UE errors. We carry LG bearing kits for same-day Edgemont service.
How does Calgary's water hardness affect Edgemont washers?+
At 150 to 200 ppm, Calgary's Bow River-sourced water deposits calcium on inlet valve screens, drum surfaces, and in the dispenser drawer over time. HE front-load washers are more affected than top-loaders because they use less water per cycle, resulting in higher mineral concentration on internal surfaces. Using the correct HE detergent dose and running a monthly maintenance cycle with washing soda helps manage scale buildup in Edgemont machines.
Can you service my older Maytag washer in Edgemont?+
Yes. Older Maytag top-loaders are robust machines and many are still worth repairing. Common issues include drive belt wear and breakage, transmission failure, and water inlet valve scale restriction. We carry belts and inlet valves for older Maytag models in stock and can typically complete the repair same day if parts are available.
