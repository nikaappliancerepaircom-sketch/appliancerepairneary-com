Refrigerator Repair Services in Marlborough

Marlborough is a well-established NE Calgary community built during the 1960s and 1970s suburban expansion north of 17th Avenue SE and east of Barlow Trail. The neighbourhood's compact bungalows and bi-levels, many of which have remained in the same family for decades, contain a wide spectrum of refrigerator vintages — from original-era GE and Whirlpool top-freezer models that have outlasted multiple kitchen renovations, to mid-era Kenmore side-by-sides purchased at the Marlborough Mall Sears, to current-generation Samsung and LG French door refrigerators installed in more recent years.

For Marlborough's older refrigerator stock, defrost system failure is the dominant repair call. GE and Whirlpool refrigerators from the 1990s and early 2000s rely on a defrost heater and defrost thermostat to prevent ice build-up on the evaporator coil. When these components fail — which they reliably do at 20-30 years of service — ice accumulates, airflow to the fresh food compartment is choked off, and the fridge gradually loses cooling while the freezer continues to function. Our technicians carry GE and Whirlpool defrost heater and thermostat kits and complete this repair in 1–1.5 hours, restoring the refrigerator to full function for $140–$220.

Kenmore refrigerators — sold exclusively through Sears and manufactured by GE, Whirlpool, LG, or Frigidaire depending on the model — are a particularly common service call in Marlborough given the neighbourhood's proximity to the former Sears at Marlborough Mall. We service all Kenmore refrigerator series, and the model number on the appliance identifies the actual manufacturer and parts sourcing before the technician arrives. For Samsung French door models with ice maker issues, and LG refrigerators potentially covered by the 10-year linear compressor warranty, we follow the same diagnostic protocol applied across all our Calgary service areas.

Fridge Not Cooling

Defrost system failures in older GE and Whirlpool units are the most common cause. Defrost heater and thermostat replacement restores cooling in one visit at $140–$220.

Ice Maker Not Working

Samsung ice maker icing issues (2012–2020 models) fixed with updated components. Kenmore ice maker modules replaced same-day. LG compressor warranty verified first.

Water Pooling Inside

Blocked defrost drain tubes cause pooling in the fresh food section. We flush and clear the drain tube and inspect the drain heater on the same visit.

Freezer Works, Fridge Warm

Failed evaporator fan motors and blocked evaporator coils (ice build-up) cause this classic symptom. We defrost manually, repair the cause, and test before leaving.

Fridge Making Noise

Condenser fan blade wear, evaporator fan bearing failure, and compressor start relay faults all cause abnormal noise. Diagnosed and repaired same-day.

Not Cooling at All

Compressor start relay failures are checked first — often a $60–$80 fix. Full compressor or sealed system diagnosis follows if the start relay is sound.
