Common Issues We Fix

Washer Problems We Repair Near You

We bring the right tools and parts to handle these common problems on our first visit to your home.

💦
Front Loader Leaking from Door

Torn or deformed door boot gasket, foreign object trapped in seal, or misaligned door hinge. Gasket replacement takes about 45 minutes.

🔴
Loud Thudding in Spin Cycle

Damaged shock absorbers or snapped suspension springs let the tub hit the cabinet. Replacement restores quiet operation immediately.

⚡
Control Panel Unresponsive

Moisture damage to the control board, failed ribbon cable, or power surge damage. We test and replace individual board components when possible.

🔍
Fills with Water but Won't Agitate

Motor coupler break, agitator dog wear, or transmission failure. Top-loader-specific issues diagnosed through mechanical inspection.

🌡
Takes Too Long to Fill

Restricted inlet valve, kinked supply hose, or low household water pressure. We measure flow rate and correct the restriction.

🚫
Burning Smell During Operation

Overheating motor, seized bearing, or belt friction. Stop immediately and call for inspection — continued use risks fire.
