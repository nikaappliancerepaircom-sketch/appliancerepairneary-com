What Are the Most Common Fridge Problems We Fix Near Toronto?

  • Fridge not cooling or cooling poorly: Defrost system failure, evaporator fan fault, or dirty condenser coils. Repair cost: $160–$280 CAD.
  • Freezer not freezing: Defrost heater, thermostat, or timer failure. Repair cost: $150–$270 CAD.
  • Fridge making loud noise: Evaporator or condenser fan blade, compressor relay. Repair cost: $140–$250 CAD.
  • Fridge leaking water: Blocked defrost drain or cracked drain pan. Repair cost: $120–$200 CAD.
  • Ice maker not working: Water inlet valve, ice maker assembly, or water line freezing. Repair cost: $150–$280 CAD.
  • Compressor failure: The most serious fridge fault. Repair cost: $350–$600 CAD depending on model.
