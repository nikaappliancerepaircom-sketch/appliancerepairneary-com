Repair vs Replace — Calgary 2026 Decision Guide

The standard rule of thumb: if the repair cost is under 50% of a comparable new appliance's price, repair almost always wins financially. Current new appliance prices in Calgary (2026):

  • Entry-level refrigerator: $900–$1,400 → repair threshold $450–$700
  • Mid-range washing machine: $700–$1,100 → repair threshold $350–$550
  • Dryer: $600–$1,000 → repair threshold $300–$500
  • Dishwasher: $600–$1,200 → repair threshold $300–$600

Based on these thresholds, the vast majority of common repairs (thermal fuses, heating elements, fans, pumps, belts) are well within repair-worthwhile range for appliances under 10 years old. Compressors and major control boards are the exceptions where you should compare repair cost to new appliance price before deciding.
