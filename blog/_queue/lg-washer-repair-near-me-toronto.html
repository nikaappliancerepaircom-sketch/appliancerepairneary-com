Most Common LG Washer Problems and Error Codes

OE Error — Drainage Failure

The most common LG washer error in Toronto. The machine won't drain. Check the drain filter (front-load machines have a small access panel at the bottom front — clean it every 3 months). If the OE persists after filter cleaning, the drain pump has failed. Repair cost: $150–$240 CAD.

LE Error — Motor Locked

The motor has detected a locked rotor condition. On LG Direct Drive models, this is often caused by a failed Hall Sensor (rotor position sensor) rather than the motor itself — a much less expensive repair. Repair cost: $160–$280 CAD.

IE Error — Water Inlet Problem

The washer isn't filling with water. Check that both hot and cold water taps are open and inlet screens aren't clogged. If water supply is confirmed, the water inlet valve assembly has failed. Repair cost: $150–$240 CAD.

UE Error — Unbalanced Load

The washer detected an unbalanced load and paused the spin cycle. Redistribute the laundry and retry. Recurring UE on balanced loads suggests worn suspension springs. Repair cost: $150–$250 CAD.

tE / tE1 — Thermistor Error

Temperature sensor failure. The washer may overheat the water or refuse to heat at all. Repair cost: $140–$230 CAD.

dE / dE1 — Door Error

The door latch isn't engaging properly. The washer won't start cycles with an open or unlocked door. Latch mechanism or switch replacement. Repair cost: $130–$220 CAD.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does OE error mean on an LG washer?
OE on an LG washer means the machine cannot drain. Check the drain filter (on front-load models, behind the access panel at the front bottom) and clear any blockage. If the OE persists, the drain pump has failed.
Why is my LG washer not spinning near Toronto?
LG front-load washers that won't spin often have a failed door latch, worn drum bearings, or a faulty rotor position sensor. Top-load models may have a worn drive motor coupling or lid switch.
Do you repair LG TurboWash and Direct Drive washers near Toronto?
Yes. We service all LG washer models including TurboWash, Direct Drive, Twin Wash, and WashTower configurations. LG Direct Drive motors have fewer moving parts but specific failure modes our technicians know well.
