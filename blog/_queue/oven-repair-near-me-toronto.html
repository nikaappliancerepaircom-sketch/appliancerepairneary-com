Gas Oven Repair Near Toronto — Why Certification Matters

Gas oven repair is not a DIY task in Ontario. Working on gas appliances requires TSSA (Technical Standards and Safety Authority) certification, which our technicians hold. Improper gas repairs create risk of gas leaks and carbon monoxide buildup — genuine safety hazards. When you book gas oven repair with us, you can be confident the work is done to Ontario safety standards, not just patched temporarily.

The most common gas oven repair we handle is igniter replacement. A failing igniter draws current but doesn't produce enough heat to open the gas valve, leaving the oven cold. This repair takes about 45 minutes and typically costs $160–$260 CAD including parts.
