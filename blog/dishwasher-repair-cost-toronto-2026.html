Dishwasher Repair Costs by Component — Toronto 2026

Drain Pump Replacement — $130–$230 CAD

The most common dishwasher repair. A failed drain pump leaves standing water in the bottom of the machine and causes error codes (IE on LG, E24 on Bosch). Drain pump access is easier on some brands than others — Bosch pumps require panel removal, slightly increasing labour time.

Water Inlet Valve — $130–$220 CAD

When the dishwasher doesn't fill with water, the inlet valve is the first suspect. Also causes water pooling under the machine when the valve doesn't close fully. Quick diagnosis, typically replaced in 30–45 minutes.

Door Gasket (Seal) — $130–$210 CAD

The rubber door seal prevents water from leaking during cycles. Torn or flattened gaskets cause water puddles on the floor in front of the dishwasher. Gasket replacement is straightforward and usually doesn't require panel removal.

Heating Element — $140–$240 CAD

The heating element heats water during the wash cycle and dries dishes at the end. When it fails, dishes come out wet and cold. Common on all major brands. Labour is moderate — the element is at the bottom of the tub.

Wash Pump / Circulation Pump — $160–$280 CAD

The wash pump circulates water through the spray arms. Failure results in dishes not being cleaned despite the machine running normally. Louder than a drain pump failure — often preceded by grinding or humming sounds.

Control Board — $200–$340 CAD

Electronic control board failure causes error codes, buttons not responding, or the machine not starting at all. Boards vary significantly in cost by brand — Bosch and Miele boards are more expensive than Whirlpool or GE equivalents.

Spray Arm Replacement — $90–$160 CAD

Clogged or cracked spray arms cause poor cleaning. Often the spray arm itself can be cleaned rather than replaced, but cracked arms must be replaced. Inexpensive repair, quick labour.

Float Switch — $110–$180 CAD

The float switch detects water levels. A stuck or failed float causes the machine to either overflow or refuse to fill. One of the simpler electrical repairs on a dishwasher.

When to Replace Instead of Repair Your Dishwasher in Toronto

Replace if: the machine is over 10–12 years old and requires a control board replacement; if multiple components fail simultaneously; if the tub or door frame is cracked or corroded beyond sealing. For all other common failures on machines under 10 years old, repair is the financially sound choice in 2026.

Pro Tip: Before calling for a dishwasher drain failure, check the filter. The filter basket at the bottom of the tub is the most overlooked maintenance item on dishwashers. A severely clogged filter mimics drain pump failure in symptoms and takes 5 minutes to clean yourself. We've made this call before — clean the filter, no service call needed.
