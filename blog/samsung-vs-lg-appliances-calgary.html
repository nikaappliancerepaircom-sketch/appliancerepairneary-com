Samsung Appliances — Calgary Failure Patterns

Samsung appliances are heavily represented in Calgary repair calls. The most common issues our technicians encounter:

Samsung Refrigerators (French Door and Side-by-Side)

The most frequently reported Samsung refrigerator problem in Calgary is ice maker failure — specifically, the ice maker freezing over due to a defrost system design issue present in many RF (French Door) models from 2015–2022. Symptoms include no ice production, excessive frost buildup in the freezer, and eventually, the fridge section warming above safe temperatures. Samsung has issued technical service bulletins on this, and the fix involves replacing the defrost sensor and adding a drain tube heater — typically $200–$320 in Calgary. The sealed system (compressor, condenser coil) on some models also fails prematurely, and compressor replacement runs $500–$800.

Samsung Washers

Samsung top-load washers have had well-publicized issues with drum separation (a safety recall on some models), as well as common UE (unbalanced) errors and door lock failures. Front-load Samsung washers are generally more reliable, though door boot seal failures and drain pump issues are common after 5–7 years in Calgary's hard water environment.

Samsung Dryers

Samsung dryers are among the more reliable products the brand makes. The most common repairs are heating element replacement ($140–$220) and thermal fuse failures — both straightforward fixes. Control board issues do appear on some models, but the overall repair rate is lower than for their refrigerators.

Calgary Tip: If you own a Samsung French door refrigerator manufactured between 2015–2021 and have never had the ice maker defrost issue repaired, run a manual defrost cycle (unplug for 24 hours) as preventive maintenance. This extends the life of the ice maker assembly.

LG Appliances — Calgary Failure Patterns

LG Refrigerators

LG refrigerators have faced a class-action lawsuit in the United States related to linear compressor failures in many InverterLinear compressor models. This issue does appear in Calgary as well — typically manifesting as the fridge gradually warming over 24–48 hours despite the compressor running. The repair involves compressor replacement ($500–$750) or a full sealed system repair. LG has extended warranties on some affected models — check your serial number at LG's website. Outside of compressor issues, LG refrigerators tend to be reliable; ice maker and cooling system problems are less frequent than Samsung's.

LG Front-Load Washers

The most common LG repair call in Calgary is drum bearing failure on front-load washers — typically presenting as a loud rumbling or grinding noise during the spin cycle. The drum bearing repair is a significant job (3–4 hours of labour) but is usually worth doing on washers under 8 years old. Calgary's hard water accelerates bearing wear because mineral deposits contaminate the bearing seal over time.

LG Dryers

LG dryers perform well overall. The most common issues are roller and drum bearing wear (presenting as squealing) and thermistor failures causing no-heat or overheating situations. Both are relatively inexpensive repairs ($120–$260).

LG Dishwashers

LG dishwashers are generally reliable, though Calgary's hard water causes accelerated scale buildup in the wash pump and spray arms. The OE error (drain error) is the most frequent service call — usually resolved by clearing the filter and checking the drain hose.
