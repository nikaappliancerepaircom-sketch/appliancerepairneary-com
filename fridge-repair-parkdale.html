Not Cooling at All

Compressor failure, refrigerant leak, or condenser fan stopped. Food safety requires diagnosis within hours — we prioritize fridge calls.

Water Pooling Inside

Clogged defrost drain, cracked drain pan, or frozen evaporator coil forcing melt water to overflow. We clear and test the drainage path.

Loud Buzzing or Clicking

Compressor struggling to start, failing start relay, or condenser fan motor bearing worn. Early repair avoids compressor burnout.

Runs Constantly Non-Stop

Compressor never shuts off — dirty coils, leaking door seal, or low refrigerant forcing continuous operation and high energy bills.
