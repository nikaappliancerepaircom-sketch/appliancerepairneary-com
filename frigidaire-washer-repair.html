Frigidaire Washer Repair — Common Issues We Fix

Our technicians have extensive experience with Frigidaire washer repair faults. We diagnose accurately and repair on the first visit in most cases.

Washer Not Starting

Frigidaire washers develop start failures from failed lid switches (top-load) or door latches (front-load), faulty control boards, or timer malfunctions in older models. We diagnose the full start circuit.

Dishwasher Drainage Issues

Frigidaire dishwashers develop drainage problems from clogged pump filters and failed drain pumps. The i30 error code indicates a drainage fault. We clear blockages and replace pumps as needed.
