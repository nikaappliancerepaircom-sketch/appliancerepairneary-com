Common Fridge Issues in Coral Springs Homes

  • Not cooling / too warm — compressor, evaporator fan, or defrost system failure; urgent food safety concern requiring same-day response
  • Ice maker not producing ice — water inlet valve scale blockage from Calgary's 150–200 ppm Bow River water is the leading cause
  • Freezer frosting over — defrost heater, bi-metal thermostat, or defrost timer failure
  • Water pooling in crisper drawers — blocked defrost drain tube; common in 1990s-era models still in service
  • Fridge running constantly — dirty condenser coils or failing door gasket allowing warm air infiltration
  • Clicking or humming noises — failing compressor start relay or condenser fan obstruction; we carry start relays for most platforms
