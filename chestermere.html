FAQ — Appliance Repair in Chestermere

Why does my fridge ice maker keep freezing in Chestermere winter? +

Chestermere's lake-community humidity is the cause. Moisture-laden air condenses inside the ice maker fill tube, then freezes overnight during cold snaps. We see this most often in Westmere, Rainbow Falls, and The Cove Samsung and LG French-door fridges. The fix is either heater wire repair (if the in-tube heater has failed) or revised assembly replacement (if the design is the issue). Typical 90-minute job, $180–$320 all-in.

Why does my front-load washer drum look corroded? +

Lake-community humidity accelerates rear drum and spider arm corrosion on front-loaders 30–40% faster than inland averages. We see Whirlpool and LG front-loaders in Westmere and Kinniburgh showing visible corrosion at year 7–9 instead of the typical 12–15. Early-stage corrosion is repairable (rear bearing + drum reseat, $380–$520 range). Late-stage spider failure usually means replacement — we lay out the math on every call.
