Common Washer Issues in Ramsay Homes

  • Not spinning or agitating — lid switch, drive belt, or motor coupling failure in top-load machines

  • UE / uE error codes — drum imbalance, suspension rod, or control board issue in Samsung units

Calgary Hard Water & Your Ramsay Washer

Ramsay's heritage homes, most of which pre-date residential water softener adoption, deliver Calgary's full 150–200 ppm mineral load directly to washer components. Front-load drum seals are the most vulnerable: they trap water after each cycle, and in hard-water conditions without regular drum cleaning, mineral deposits combine with detergent residue to create a persistent biofilm that degrades the rubber and causes both musty odour and eventually seal failure. Our technicians clean and treat drum seals on Ramsay service calls involving front-load washers, and advise clients on monthly cleaning cycles calibrated to Calgary's specific water hardness — preventing a recurring maintenance problem from becoming a part-replacement cost.

Local tip for Ramsay homeowners: In Ramsay's compact heritage homes where the washer is in a basement utility room with limited ventilation, the risk of drum seal deterioration is higher. After every front-load wash cycle, leave the door slightly ajar to allow the drum to dry. This single habit significantly extends door seal life in Calgary's hard-water conditions.
