Common Issues We Fix

Oven Problems We Repair Near You in Bayview Village

From simple fixes to complex repairs, we handle every issue below with same-day parts and expert diagnosis.

🔍
Takes Forever to Preheat

Weak igniter drawing current but not reaching ignition temperature, hidden element degradation, or insulation loss around the cavity.

🌡
Broiler Element Not Working

Upper element open-circuit, broiler relay on the control board failed, or selector switch not routing power to broil mode.

🔴
Convection Fan Noisy or Stopped

Fan motor bearing worn, blade hitting the back panel, or motor winding burned out. Convection cooking requires even airflow.

⚡
F1 or F3 Fault Code

F1 indicates control board failure; F3 indicates open or shorted oven temperature sensor. We test the sensor circuit and replace the failed part.

🚫
Hinges Broken — Door Drops

Hinge springs snapped, hinge arm bent, or mounting bracket cracked. Door weight makes operation dangerous until repaired.

💦
Steam or Moisture During Baking

Door gasket not sealing, excessive moisture from food, or vent tube blocked. Some steam is normal — excessive indicates a seal issue.
