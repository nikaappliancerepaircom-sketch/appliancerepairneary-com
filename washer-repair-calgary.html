Common Washer Repair Problems We Fix

💦
Slow Leak — Water on Floor

Gradual tub seal wear, weeping hose clamp, or hairline crack in the outer tub. We inspect every potential leak point systematically.

🔴
Metallic Scraping Sound

A coin, underwire, or button caught between the drum and tub. Left unattended it scratches the drum surface permanently.

⚡
Blinking Lights — No Start

The control board is in diagnostic mode or has detected a fault before allowing the cycle to begin. We read the blink pattern to identify the issue.

🔍
Drum Doesn't Rotate

Broken belt, seized motor, or failed motor coupling. We identify whether the blockage is mechanical or electrical.

🌡
Cold Rinse Only — No Hot

Hot inlet valve solenoid has failed while cold continues working. The washer fills with cold water regardless of temperature setting.

🚫
Lid Won't Lock (Top Loader)

Actuator failure, lid switch break, or wiring harness damage. The safety interlock prevents the spin cycle from engaging.
