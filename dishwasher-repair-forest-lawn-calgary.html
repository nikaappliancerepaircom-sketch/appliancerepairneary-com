Calgary Hard Water & Your Forest Lawn Dishwasher

Forest Lawn's high-density rental properties deliver Calgary's 150–200 ppm Bow River water to dishwashers running two or three cycles per day — an accelerated mineral load that produces visible calcium buildup on spray arms and heating elements within a year or two of installation. In properties without water softeners, the heating element may develop a calcium coating significant enough to degrade drying performance before the element fails outright. Our technicians perform in-visit descaling on Forest Lawn service calls where spray arm blockage or heating element coating is found, and advise tenants and property managers on the rinse-aid dosing levels and dishwasher cleaner schedules appropriate for Calgary's water conditions in high-use rental environments.

Tip for Forest Lawn property managers: In rental properties running dishwashers daily, schedule a descaling service every 18–24 months regardless of visible problems. Proactive maintenance on Forest Lawn rental units costs significantly less than emergency drain pump or wash motor replacement — and avoids tenant complaints during the repair window.
