Common Dishwasher Problems in MacEwan Glen

  • GE wash pump motor wear — the motor shaft bearing in older GE Profile dishwashers wears unevenly, causing a grinding noise that worsens during the main wash cycle
  • GE drain check valve fouling — food debris lodges in the one-way valve at the pump outlet, causing standing water to remain after the drain cycle
  • Whirlpool door latch crack — the plastic latch body on Whirlpool machines from the 1990s becomes brittle and cracks, causing the door to spring open during the wash cycle
  • Whirlpool control board relay failure — electrolytic capacitors on older Whirlpool control boards dry out, causing random cycle interruptions or failure to start
  • Samsung touchpad dead zones — the capacitive touchpad overlay separates from the control board connector, creating unresponsive button areas on Samsung machines
  • Hard water spray arm scaling — Calgary water at 150–200 ppm deposits calcite in dishwasher spray arm nozzles within 18–24 months without regular rinse aid, reducing wash pressure and leaving dishes spotted

Hard Water Maintenance for MacEwan Glen Dishwashers

With Calgary water hardness in the 150–200 ppm range, MacEwan Glen dishwashers running without rinse aid show visible limescale build-up within two years. The spray arm micro-nozzles clog progressively — first the smallest nozzles on the upper arm, then the larger lower arm ports — reducing wash coverage from the bottom up. Our technicians perform a full descale and nozzle check on every MacEwan Glen service call, removing existing deposits with a citric acid treatment and advising on the correct rinse aid dosage setting for the machine's water softener (where fitted). Preventing reoccurrence is as important to us as fixing the immediate fault.
