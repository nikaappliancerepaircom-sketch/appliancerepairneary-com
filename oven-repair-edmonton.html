Common Oven Repair Problems We Fix

🌡
Pilot Light Keeps Going Out

Thermocouple weak, pilot orifice clogged, or draft blowing out the flame. Thermocouple replacement is quick and inexpensive.

🔴
Banging When Door Closes

Hinge slam from broken door spring or dampener, or loose inner panel. We adjust and replace springs to restore smooth closure.

🔍
Timer Beeps But Oven Won't Stop

Relay on control board stuck in the on position, or timer motor disconnected from the control circuit. The oven ignores the end signal.

⚡
Preheats Then Shows Error

Temperature sensor reads correctly at room temp but fails under heat — cracked ceramic insulation or wire break that opens at high temperature.

🚫
Oven Door Glass Cracked

Inner or outer glass panel cracked from thermal shock or impact. We source replacement glass panels for all major oven brands.

💦
Food Tastes Like Gas

Incomplete combustion from clogged burner ports, low gas pressure, or flame not adjusted properly. Burner service resolves the issue.
