Maintenance Tips for Refrigerator Owners in Brampton

Preventive maintenance extends the life of your refrigerator and reduces the likelihood of emergency repairs. Brampton sits slightly inland from Lake Ontario, experiencing colder winters and hotter summers than lakefront communities — this temperature range stresses appliance compressors and heating elements. Here are five maintenance practices we recommend specifically for Brampton homeowners:

  1. Clean the condenser coils every six months — pull the fridge away from the wall or remove the bottom grille, and vacuum or brush away dust and pet hair that forces the compressor to work harder and shortens its life
  2. Check the door gasket seal monthly by closing the door on a piece of paper — if the paper slides out easily, the gasket is not sealing properly and should be cleaned or replaced to maintain efficiency
  3. Keep the refrigerator temperature at 3-4°C and the freezer at -18°C — temperatures outside this range waste energy, compromise food safety, or cause frost buildup that stresses the defrost system
  4. Clear the area around the refrigerator for proper ventilation — maintain at least 2 inches of clearance on each side and behind the unit to allow heat dissipation from the condenser coils
  5. Clean the drain pan and defrost drain twice a year — remove the drain pan (usually accessible from the bottom front), wash it with mild soap, and flush the defrost drain with warm water to prevent clogs and odour

Following these steps can add years to your refrigerator's lifespan and prevent the most common repair calls we see in Brampton. If you notice anything unusual despite regular maintenance, call (437) 524-1053 — early diagnosis saves money.
