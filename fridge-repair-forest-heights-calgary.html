Refrigerator Repair Services in Forest Heights

Forest Heights homeowners have been loyal to their appliances the way they've been loyal to their neighbourhood — for decades. The 1960s-70s built homes in this inner SE community contain a wide range of refrigerator vintages, and our technicians encounter everything from original-era Frigidaire single-door models that have outlasted the homes' original owners, to mid-2000s Kenmore and GE side-by-sides installed during kitchen renovations, to the latest Samsung and LG French door models purchased in the last five to ten years.

The most common fridge failure pattern in Forest Heights follows a predictable generational arc. Frigidaire and GE refrigerators from the 1990s and 2000s — now 20-30 years old — most often present with defrost system failures. The defrost heater burns out, ice accumulates on the evaporator coil, and the fridge gradually loses its ability to cool the fresh food compartment while the freezer continues to function normally. This classic symptom pattern is one of the easiest fridge repairs we perform: we access the evaporator panel, replace the heater and thermostat, manually clear the accumulated ice, and the fridge returns to normal within hours. Repair cost: $140–$220, compared to $1,200–$2,000 for a replacement of similar capacity.

For Forest Heights homes with Samsung French door refrigerators, the ice maker assembly is a recurring service call. The known design issue with water supply tube freezing in certain 2012–2020 models causes ice production to stop entirely and can result in water pooling in the fresh food section. We carry the updated parts and complete the fix in 1–1.5 hours. LG linear compressor warranty coverage (10 years from purchase for qualifying models) is always verified before we recommend any compressor-related repair.

Fridge Not Cooling (Freezer OK)

Samsung French door ice maker icing issues (2012–2020 models) fixed with updated tube clips and modules. LG warranty checked before recommending compressor work.

Water Pooling Inside

Blocked defrost drain tubes cause water to accumulate at the bottom of the refrigerator compartment. We flush and clear the drain tube and check the drain heater.

Fridge Too Warm or Too Cold

Temperature control thermostat failures, damper motor faults, and dirty condenser coils cause temperature instability. Full diagnosis performed on-site.

Making Unusual Noises

Noisy evaporator and condenser fans, and failing compressor start relays are common in ageing units. Fan motors and start relays replaced same-day.

Compressor Not Running

Frequently Asked Questions — Forest Heights Fridge Repair

My fridge isn't cooling but the freezer is fine — what's wrong?

This is the classic symptom of a defrost system failure. Ice builds up on the evaporator coil, blocking airflow to the fresh food section while the freezer continues to function. The fix involves replacing the defrost heater and thermostat — a $140–$220 repair that's one of the most cost-effective fridge repairs we perform.

Is a 25-year-old Frigidaire fridge worth repairing?

If the repair is a defrost system fix ($140–$220) or fan motor replacement ($130–$200), yes — these repairs offer good value on a machine that's otherwise structurally sound. If the sealed refrigerant system or compressor has failed on a 25-year-old unit, replacement is typically the better economic choice. We'll tell you honestly which situation applies.

How do I know if my LG compressor is still under warranty?

LG's linear compressor carries a 10-year warranty from the original purchase date for qualifying models manufactured between 2014 and 2022. We verify eligibility using the model and serial number before recommending any compressor repair. If your unit qualifies, warranty repairs are processed at no parts cost to you.
