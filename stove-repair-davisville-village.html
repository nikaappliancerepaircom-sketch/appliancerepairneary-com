Stove Problems We Fix in Davisville Village

Only Two of Four Burners Work

Independent circuits per burner pair — one leg of the 240V supply may be lost at the breaker, outlet, or terminal block.

🌡
Pilot Light Won't Stay Lit

Thermocouple not in the flame path, weak thermocouple signal, or draft blowing out the pilot. Quick thermocouple replacement fixes it.

🔴
Rattling Inside Range Body

Loose insulation, displaced heat shield, or oven fan bracket vibrating. We access the interior and secure all components.

⚡
Self-Clean Lock Engaged — Won't Open

Lock motor failed mid-cycle, thermal fuse protecting the lock circuit blown, or control board stuck in clean mode.

🚫
Control Knob Gets Extremely Hot

Heat traveling up the valve stem from the burner, missing stem gasket, or poor insulation around the manifold. Safety concern.

💦
Water Under Range After Mopping

Water seeped under the range during floor cleaning, pooling around gas connections or electrical terminals. Dry and inspect before using.

Warning Signs — Call Immediately in Davisville Village

  • Gas smell when all burners are off — potential gas leak. Open windows, leave the home, call your gas utility immediately.
  • Yellow or orange flame instead of blue — incomplete combustion producing carbon monoxide. Needs immediate attention.
  • Oven igniter takes more than 90 seconds — weakening igniter. Replace before complete failure.
  • Constant clicking after burners off — spark module short or moisture intrusion. Fire hazard.
  • Cracked glass cooktop with visible element — shock hazard. Do not use until repaired.
