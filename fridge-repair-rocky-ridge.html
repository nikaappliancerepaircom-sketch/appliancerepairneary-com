Samsung French door refrigerators are the dominant high-volume fridge service call in Rocky Ridge. The community's 2005 to 2015 Samsung units most commonly develop ice maker defrost heater failures — the heater that prevents ice buildup on the ice maker evaporator shorts out over time, and ice accumulates until the ejector mechanism is completely blocked. The symptom is that the ice maker runs through its cycle motions but no cubes drop into the bin — the ejector arms are frozen in place. Frequently Asked Questions — Rocky Ridge Fridge Repair

My Rocky Ridge Samsung fridge ice maker runs but drops no cubes — what's wrong?+
When the ice maker motor runs through its cycle but no cubes drop, the ejector arms are almost always frozen in place by ice buildup around the ice maker evaporator. This occurs when the ice maker defrost heater has failed — the heater that prevents ice from accumulating around the evaporator coil shorts out over time. Replacing the ice maker assembly (which includes the heater and thermostat) resolves this permanently. We carry Samsung ice maker assemblies for same-day Rocky Ridge service.
My Rocky Ridge fridge is slowly getting warmer over several days — is that the defrost system?+
Yes. A gradual 2 to 3 day temperature rise — as opposed to a sudden failure — is the characteristic symptom of defrost system failure. When the defrost heater or defrost thermostat fails, frost accumulates on the evaporator coil over 24 to 48 hours, eventually insulating it so thoroughly that the coil can no longer absorb heat from the refrigerator interior. The fridge warms slowly from the inside. Defrost heater and thermostat replacement restores normal operation immediately.
Is my Rocky Ridge LG refrigerator compressor covered under warranty?+
LG linear compressors carry a 10-year warranty in Canada. Rocky Ridge homes built from 2010 onward frequently feature LG French door and bottom-freezer refrigerators purchased between 2012 and 2018 — models that may still be within the compressor warranty window. We verify the model, serial number, and purchase date during the diagnostic visit and advise on warranty eligibility before any repair cost is confirmed.
How does Calgary's hard water affect my Rocky Ridge refrigerator?+
Calgary's Bow River water at 150 to 200 ppm calcium carbonate deposits scale on the water inlet valve solenoid screens over years of use. In a Rocky Ridge refrigerator at 10 to 15 years of age, this scale progressively restricts water flow to the ice maker — initially producing smaller cubes, then reducing ice output, then stopping production. Using a refrigerator water filter on a 6-month replacement schedule intercepts some hardness minerals before they reach the valve, extending valve service life in Calgary's hard water climate.
