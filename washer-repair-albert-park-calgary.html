The most common washer brand calls we receive from Albert Park homes are Kenmore (the Sears-era Whirlpool-built top-loaders that dominated this neighbourhood through the 1980s and 1990s), GE top-load machines, and newer Samsung and LG front-loaders installed by landlords during renovations. Rental-grade Samsung front-loaders commonly present with 5E/SE drain errors caused by coin trap blockages from tenants' clothing, which we clear and test in under an hour.

We stock a broad range of Kenmore, Whirlpool, GE, Samsung, and LG washer parts at the vehicle level, ensuring most Albert Park repairs are completed on the first visit without a return trip for parts.

Won't Spin or Agitate

Worn drive belts, failed lid switches, and transmission coupling failures are common in older Kenmore and GE top-loaders. We diagnose and replace in one visit.

Won't Drain (5E Error)

Samsung 5E and LG OE drain errors often trace to blocked coin filters or failed drain pumps. Common in rental units — resolved quickly without major disassembly.

Leaking Water

Door gasket tears on front-loaders and tub seal failures on top-loaders cause floor leaks. We carry gaskets for major brands in stock.

Excessive Vibration / Noise

Worn drum bearings, failing shock absorbers, and unbalanced drum spider arms cause banging and excessive movement. We inspect and replace worn components.

Won't Start / No Power

Faulty lid switches (top-load), door latch assemblies (front-load), and failed control boards prevent the machine from starting. Diagnosed with manufacturer tools.

Not Filling with Water

Failed water inlet valves are a common failure on machines over 15 years old. We replace dual-port inlet valve assemblies and test fill cycles before leaving.

Frequently Asked Questions — Albert Park Washer Repair

Do you work in basement laundry rooms and older apartment buildings?

Yes. Albert Park's older housing stock often features basement laundry rooms with limited access. Our technicians are experienced working in tight spaces and bring all tools and parts needed. We complete the repair in one visit in the vast majority of cases.

My Kenmore top-loader won't spin — is it worth fixing?

Kenmore top-loaders from the Sears era (Whirlpool-built) are known for durability. A failed spin cycle is usually caused by a worn drive belt or coupling — repairs that cost $110–$180 and restore full function. If the machine is structurally sound, repair is almost always better value than replacement.

My Samsung washer shows a 5E error — what does it mean?

The 5E (or SE) error code indicates a drain failure. The most common cause is a blocked coin/debris filter, which is accessible from the front panel without tools. We also check the drain pump and drain hose for kinks or blockages. This is one of our fastest fixes — usually resolved within 45–60 minutes.
