Won't Spin or Agitate

Worn drive belts, failed lid switches, and transmission coupling failures are common in older Kenmore and GE top-loaders. We diagnose and replace in one visit.

Won't Drain (5E Error)

Samsung 5E and LG OE drain errors often trace to blocked coin filters or failed drain pumps. Common in rental units — resolved quickly without major disassembly.

Leaking Water

Door gasket tears on front-loaders and tub seal failures on top-loaders cause floor leaks. We carry gaskets for major brands in stock.

Excessive Vibration / Noise

Worn drum bearings, failing shock absorbers, and unbalanced drum spider arms cause banging and excessive movement. We inspect and replace worn components.

Won't Start / No Power

Faulty lid switches (top-load), door latch assemblies (front-load), and failed control boards prevent the machine from starting. Diagnosed with manufacturer tools.

Not Filling with Water

Failed water inlet valves are a common failure on machines over 15 years old. We replace dual-port inlet valve assemblies and test fill cycles before leaving.

My Samsung washer shows a 5E error — what does it mean?

The 5E (or SE) error code indicates a drain failure. The most common cause is a blocked coin/debris filter, which is accessible from the front panel without tools. Washer Care Guide for Albert Park Calgary Homeowners

A well-maintained washer lasts 12–15 years. One that is not typically fails at 7–9 years. Calgary's hard water (150–200 ppm from the Bow River) and temperature cycling create extra wear — here is how Albert Park Calgary homeowners can extend their washer's life based on the repairs our technicians see most often:

  • Monthly: Run a hot maintenance cycle with a washer cleaner tablet (Affresh or equivalent). This removes detergent residue and mineral deposits that accumulate in the drum and pump filter from Calgary's hard water supply.
  • Monthly: Leave the front-loader door ajar between cycles to prevent mould growth in the drum. Wipe the door boot gasket with a damp cloth — this is where most front-loader odours and seal deterioration begin in Albert Park Calgary homes.
  • Quarterly: Check hose connections at the back of the unit. Calgary's Chinook temperature swings can stress hose connections — slow leaks at the connection point often go undetected until they cause floor damage.
  • Always: Use HE detergent in HE washers. Standard detergent over-suds in low-water machines and leaves residue that accelerates drum bearing wear — one of the most common causes of the grinding noise we repair in Albert Park Calgary homes.

Common Washer Problems We Repair in Albert Park Calgary

Washing machines in Albert Park Calgary face a combination of regular wear and Calgary-specific challenges — hard water mineral buildup from the Bow River supply at 150–200 ppm, and Calgary's Chinook temperature swings that stress seals and hoses throughout the year. These are the washer faults our technicians see most often:

  • →Drum not spinning — a failed lid switch (top-loaders) or door latch (front-loaders) prevents the spin cycle from engaging. Common on HE washers over 5 years old in Albert Park Calgary homes.
  • →Excessive vibration or grinding during spin — worn drum bearings are the usual cause. A bearing caught early (grinding noise) costs $200–$380 to fix; left untreated, the drum shaft fails and costs significantly more.
  • →Water not draining after the cycle — a blocked pump filter or failed drain pump. Front-loaders are particularly prone to small-item blockages in Albert Park Calgary laundry rooms.
  • →Water leaking onto the floor — door boot seals on front-loaders and hose connections at the back of the unit. Calgary's hard water and temperature cycling accelerate seal deterioration.
  • →F21, E3, or UL error codes — these indicate specific component failures. We diagnose and resolve with a definitive repair, not a reset workaround.

Most Albert Park Calgary washer repairs are resolved in one visit. Call us at the first grinding noise — a bearing caught early saves you from a much more expensive drum shaft replacement later.
