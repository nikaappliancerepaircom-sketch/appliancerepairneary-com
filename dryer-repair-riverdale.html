Common Issues We Fix

Dryer Problems We Repair Near You in Riverdale

We bring the right tools and parts to handle these common problems on our first visit to your home.

🔴
Loud Pop When Starting

Belt tension snap, motor start winding engaging hard, or loose blower wheel hitting housing. We identify the source and eliminate the noise.

🌡
Overheating — Clothes Too Hot

Cycling thermostat stuck closed, blocked exhaust vent, or failed high-limit thermostat. Overheating damages clothes and risks fire.

🔍
Display Shows Time But Won't Run

Start relay on the control board failed, motor capacitor dead, or safety circuit open. Electronic diagnostics pinpoint the issue.

⚡
Stops and Restarts Randomly

Intermittent door switch, loose wiring connection, or control board relay chattering. Inconsistent operation wastes energy and time.

💦
Vent Flap Won't Open Outside

Bird nest, lint accumulation, or frozen condensation blocking the exterior vent cap. We clean and test the full vent from dryer to exit.

🚫
Timer Knob Spins Freely

Broken timer shaft or stripped timer knob coupling. The timer dial turns but doesn't engage the timer mechanism inside.
