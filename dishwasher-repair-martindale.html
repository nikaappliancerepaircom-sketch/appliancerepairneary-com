Calgary Hard Water & Martindale Dishwashers

Calgary's Bow River water at 150–200 ppm is classified as hard to very hard. In Martindale homes from the mid-1990s and early 2000s, many built without water softeners and now 20–30 years into service, the cumulative mineral build-up in dishwasher components is substantial.
