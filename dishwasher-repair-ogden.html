Common Dishwasher Issues in Ogden

  • Drain pump failure — standing water after cycle, common in aging Kenmore and GE units
  • Hard water scale on spray arms — 150–200 ppm Calgary water blocks nozzles, causing poor wash results
  • Control board faults — dishwasher won't start or stops mid-cycle
  • Door latch and gasket failure — water leaking from door seals in older machines
  • Water inlet valve restriction — slow filling or no fill, caused by mineral scale buildup
  • Heating element failure — dishes not drying, detergent pods not dissolving fully

Repair vs. Replace in Ogden

Ogden's older housing stock means many homeowners face this decision on machines that are 15–25 years old. Our rule: if repair cost is under 50% of a comparable new dishwasher and the tub and racks are sound, repair is almost always the smarter financial choice.
