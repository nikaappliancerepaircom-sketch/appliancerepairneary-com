Calgary's Bow River water at 150 to 200 ppm calcium carbonate hardness creates a predictable set of ice maker failures in Royal Oak refrigerators. The water inlet valve — the solenoid-controlled valve that supplies water to both the ice maker and dispenser — accumulates scale on its internal screens and valve seats over years of use. At 15 to 20 years of service, this scale often reaches the point where water flow restriction is severe enough to stop ice production entirely and reduce dispenser flow to a trickle. In newer LG and Samsung models, a water filter helps intercept some hardness before the valve; in older units without filtration, valve replacement is a routine maintenance repair in the Calgary hard water environment. We carry inlet valve assemblies for the dominant Royal Oak refrigerator brands for same-day service.

Samsung French door refrigerators in Royal Oak most commonly develop ice maker defrost heater failures — the defrost heater that prevents ice from building up around the ice maker evaporator shorts out over time, and ice eventually blocks the ejector mechanism completely. Samsung ice maker assembly replacement resolves this and is a same-day repair. LG French door and bottom-freezer refrigerators with linear compressors — widely sold from 2014 onward — carry a 10-year Canadian compressor warranty; we verify eligibility during the diagnostic visit. Whirlpool and KitchenAid side-by-side refrigerators in the community commonly develop the evaporator fan motor failure presenting as a warm fresh food compartment with a normal freezer — the fan that circulates cold air from the freezer has seized, and replacement restores the temperature differential immediately.

Frequently Asked Questions — Royal Oak Fridge Repair

Why did my Royal Oak Samsung fridge stop making ice?+
The three most common causes in Royal Oak Samsung French door refrigerators are: (1) a failed water inlet valve with calcium scale restricting water flow to the ice maker; (2) a defrost heater failure in the ice maker compartment allowing ice to build up and block the ejector; and (3) a failed ice maker module. Our $65 diagnostic identifies which of these has occurred before any parts are ordered.
My Royal Oak fridge is warm but the freezer is cold — what does that mean?+
This pattern — warm fresh food compartment with a normal freezer — almost always indicates evaporator fan motor failure. The fan circulates cold air from the freezer into the fresh food section; when the motor seizes, the freezer maintains temperature from direct compressor cooling, but the fresh food section warms up. Evaporator fan motor replacement is a same-day repair in most Whirlpool, KitchenAid, Samsung, and LG configurations.
Is my LG fridge in Royal Oak still under compressor warranty?+
LG linear compressors carry a 10-year warranty in Canada from the original purchase date. If your Royal Oak LG refrigerator was purchased between 2014 and 2020, the compressor may be covered. We verify the serial number and purchase date during the diagnostic visit and advise you on warranty status before recommending any repair cost for a compressor-related issue.
How does Calgary's water hardness affect my Royal Oak fridge?+
Calgary's Bow River water at 150 to 200 ppm calcium carbonate deposits scale on the water inlet valve solenoid screens over time. This progressively restricts water flow to the ice maker and dispenser — first producing small cubes and slow dispensing, eventually stopping production entirely. Using a refrigerator water filter rated for hardness reduction and replacing it on schedule (every 6 months) meaningfully extends inlet valve life in Royal Oak's hard water environment.
