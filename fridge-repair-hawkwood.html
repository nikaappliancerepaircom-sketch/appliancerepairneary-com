Fridge Repair in Hawkwood — Reliable Service for NW Calgary's Mature Suburb

Hawkwood's family homes, built predominantly in the 1980s and early 1990s, are now entering their third or fourth decade of occupancy. Fridges in these homes span a wide vintage range — from 1990s GE and Frigidaire units that have been running for 30 years, to 2000s-era Whirlpool and Kenmore side-by-sides, and more recent Samsung and LG French door models installed during kitchen renovations. We reach the community in approximately 28 minutes from our 700 6th Ave SW Calgary base via Sarcee Trail NW and Crowfoot Trail NW, making same-day response reliable for this established NW suburb.

Calgary's Bow River water at 150 to 200 ppm hardness creates specific long-term damage in Hawkwood refrigerators equipped with ice makers and water dispensers. The water inlet valve — a solenoid-controlled valve that feeds both the ice maker and dispenser — accumulates calcium deposits on the valve seats and screens over years of use. This scale progressively restricts water flow, causing the ice maker to produce small or hollow cubes and eventually stopping production altogether. In 20 to 30 year old GE and Frigidaire refrigerators, the inlet valve is typically a straightforward repair; in these machines, we also inspect the condenser coils — which in older top-freezer models are mounted on the exterior back of the cabinet and collect dust in Hawkwood's dry continental climate, reducing cooling efficiency by 20 to 30 percent when heavily coated. Compressor start relay failure is the most common cause of complete cooling loss in older GE and Frigidaire compressors; the relay is an inexpensive component that we carry for same-day replacement.

Samsung French door refrigerators in Hawkwood — typically installed during 2005 to 2015 kitchen renovations — most frequently develop ice maker failures caused by a defrost heater fault on the ice maker assembly. Ice builds up around the ice maker, preventing the ejector from releasing cubes; the fix involves replacing the defrost heater and thermostat in the ice maker compartment. LG refrigerators with linear compressors — widely sold from 2014 onward — carry a 10-year compressor warranty in Canada; if your Hawkwood LG fridge is cooling poorly and the compressor is within warranty, we verify eligibility during the diagnostic visit. Whirlpool and KitchenAid refrigerators in the community commonly develop evaporator fan motor failure — the fan that circulates cold air from the freezer into the fresh food compartment — presenting as a warm fridge with a normal-temperature freezer. Fan motor replacement is a same-day repair in most configurations.

Frequently Asked Questions — Hawkwood Fridge Repair

Why is my Hawkwood fridge running but not cooling?+
The most common causes are a failed compressor start relay, a seized evaporator fan motor, or condenser coils blocked by dust. In older GE and Frigidaire units common in Hawkwood, the start relay fails most frequently — it's an inexpensive component and a quick repair. In Whirlpool and Samsung units, the evaporator fan is the more likely culprit. Our $65 diagnostic identifies the cause before any parts are ordered.
Does hard water damage my Hawkwood refrigerator?+
Yes, specifically the ice maker and water dispenser. Calgary's Bow River water at 150 to 200 ppm calcium carbonate deposits scale on the water inlet valve solenoid screens and valve seats over years of use. This progressively restricts water flow to the ice maker, causing small or hollow cubes before stopping production entirely. A new inlet valve restores full water flow and ice production.
My LG fridge in Hawkwood is not cooling well — is the compressor covered under warranty?+
LG linear compressors carry a 10-year warranty in Canada. If your LG refrigerator was purchased after 2014 and is showing cooling problems, the compressor may still be covered. We verify the serial number and purchase date during the diagnostic visit and advise you on warranty eligibility before recommending any paid repair.
Is it worth repairing a 20-year-old fridge in Hawkwood?+
Often yes, if the repair is a single component failure. A compressor start relay or evaporator fan replacement on a 20-year-old GE or Frigidaire is a sensible repair if the cabinet seals, shelving, and compressor are all otherwise sound. If the compressor itself has failed on a 20+ year unit, replacement is usually the better economic choice. Our diagnostic gives you full information to make that call.
