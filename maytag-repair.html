Common Maytag Problems We See in Toronto

The Maytag Bravos washer series is one of the most popular top-load washers in GTA homes, and we repair them daily. The most frequent Bravos complaint is excessive vibration and banging during the spin cycle. This is typically caused by worn suspension rods or a failing tub bearing. If left unaddressed, a damaged bearing can destroy the outer tub, turning a $250 repair into a $700 problem. We recommend addressing vibration issues as soon as they appear.

Maytag dryers built between 2012 and 2020 commonly develop heating element failures that leave clothes damp after a full cycle. In gas dryer models, the problem is often a failing gas valve solenoid rather than the igniter itself. Our technicians test each component in sequence — thermal fuse, high-limit thermostat, igniter resistance, and gas valve continuity — to identify the exact failed part and avoid unnecessary replacements.

Maytag dishwashers with the PowerBlast cycle feature are generally excellent performers, but the chopper blade assembly at the bottom of the sump can wear out after several years of heavy use. When it does, food particles circulate back through the wash system and redeposit on dishes. Our technicians replace the chopper blade and clean the entire filtration pathway to restore full cleaning performance.

The Maytag Neptune front-load washer, although discontinued, is still found in many Toronto homes. These units are known for developing door boot seal mold and bearing failures that produce a grinding noise during spin. We carry Neptune-specific parts and can complete both of these common repairs in a single appointment.
