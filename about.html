Our Story

Rooted in the Community, Serving Your Neighbourhood

Appliance Repair Near Me began in 2015 when our founder — a certified appliance technician with over a decade of field experience — noticed that residents across the GTA were struggling to find reliable, prompt repair services without paying inflated "big company" prices.

The solution was straightforward: build a team of skilled local technicians stationed throughout Toronto, Scarborough, North York, Etobicoke, Mississauga, Brampton, and beyond. By keeping our team genuinely local, we can be at your door faster than any centralized service.

Today we have completed over 5,200 appliance repairs across the Greater Toronto Area. That local knowledge translates into faster service and better results for you.
