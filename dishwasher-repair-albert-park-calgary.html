Dishwasher Repair Services in Albert Park

Albert Park is one of Calgary's established inner-SE neighbourhoods, developed primarily through the 1940s and 1950s as workers and tradespeople settled east of the Elbow River. The area has a strong working-class heritage, with compact bungalows and modest semi-detached homes lining tidy avenues. Today Albert Park has a high proportion of rental housing and long-term owner-occupiers who have maintained their homes — and their appliances — for decades. That means our technicians regularly service 25-to-40-year-old Kenmore and GE dishwashers alongside newer Bosch and Samsung units installed by landlords or newer residents undertaking renovations.

Calgary's hard-water supply (150–200 ppm calcium hardness) accelerates limescale formation in dishwasher spray arms, wash pumps, and heating elements — a problem that affects every neighbourhood in the city equally. In Albert Park, older Kenmore and GE units that predate modern descaling cycles are especially prone to spray arm clogging and reduced wash temperatures. Many residents call us expecting a full replacement when a thorough descale and pump service is all that's needed, saving hundreds of dollars on an appliance that otherwise has years of service remaining.

We carry a comprehensive stock of OEM and high-quality aftermarket parts for the most common Albert Park brands — Kenmore rack hardware, GE door latch assemblies, Whirlpool control boards, and Bosch circulation pumps — so most repairs are completed in a single visit. Our 90-day parts and labour warranty applies to all work, and our flat-rate diagnostic fee is waived when you proceed with the repair.

Not Cleaning Dishes

Clogged spray arms, worn wash pump impellers, or blocked filters — very common in older Kenmore and GE units with years of hard-water mineral build-up.

Won't Drain Properly

Drain pump failures and blocked drain hoses are a top call for 1980s-90s-era dishwashers. We diagnose and replace drain pumps in one visit.

Door Latch / Won't Start

Faulty door latch assemblies and failed control boards are frequent on older GE and Kenmore models. We carry latch kits and compatible boards in stock.

Water Leaks

Deteriorated door gaskets and cracked spray arm hubs allow water onto the kitchen floor. We replace seals and hose assemblies same-day.

Not Filling with Water

Failed inlet solenoid valves or float switch faults prevent the tub from filling. Common on 15-25-year-old units with original valves still in service.

Error Codes (Bosch / Samsung)

Bosch E24/E25 drain errors and Samsung LC/OC codes are diagnosed with manufacturer tools. We clear the fault and address the root cause.

My Kenmore dishwasher is 30+ years old — is it worth repairing?

Older Kenmore dishwashers (especially Sears-era Whirlpool-built models) are genuinely durable. If the repair cost is under 50% of a comparable new unit and the tub and door are structurally sound, repair is usually the better value — especially when parts are still readily available. We'll give you an honest assessment before any work begins.

Does Calgary's hard water affect my dishwasher?

Yes. Calgary's municipal water supply measures 150–200 ppm calcium hardness, which is considered moderately hard to hard. This causes limescale to accumulate in spray arms, filters, and heating elements over time. Using a quality dishwasher detergent with a built-in rinse aid and periodically running a descaling cycle with citric acid will significantly extend the life of your appliance.
