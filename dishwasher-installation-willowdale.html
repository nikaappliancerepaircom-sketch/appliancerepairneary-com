Here is exactly what is included in every installation:

  • Step 1: Disconnect and remove the old dishwasher — We safely disconnect the water supply, drain line, and electrical connection from your old unit. The old dishwasher is hauled away and disposed of responsibly so you do not have to arrange separate removal.
  • Step 2: Prepare the cabinet opening — We inspect the opening for any obstructions, verify the dimensions match your new unit, and ensure the flooring underneath is level and undamaged before positioning.

What We Handle During Installation

🔍
Not Cleaning Dishes

Clogged spray arms, weak wash motor, or detergent dispenser failure. We restore full cleaning performance on the first visit.

💦
Leaking from Bottom

Worn door gasket, cracked sump, or loose hose clamp. We locate the leak source and repair it before water damages your flooring.

⚡
Error Codes Flashing

Bosch E15, Samsung LC, LG AE — we decode manufacturer fault codes on-site and repair the underlying pump, sensor, or board issue.

🔴
Grinding or Humming Sounds

Foreign object in the pump, worn wash motor bearings, or jammed chopper blade. Early repair prevents pump motor burnout.

🌡
Not Draining After Cycle

Blocked drain filter, failed drain pump, or kinked drain hose. We clear the blockage and verify proper drainage flow.

🚫
Door Won't Latch Closed

Broken latch mechanism, misaligned strike plate, or warped tub. The safety interlock prevents operation until the door seals.
