Common Dishwasher Issues in Monterey Park Homes

  • Hard-water scale blocking spray arms — Calgary's 150–200 ppm Bow River water clogs nozzles within 2–4 years without regular descaling
  • Not draining / standing water — drain pump or check valve failure, often accelerated by mineral deposits on the impeller
  • Door gasket and latch failure — common in 15–20 year-old units; Calgary freeze-thaw cycles accelerate rubber deterioration
  • Heating element burnout — calcium coating insulates the element, driving up energy use before it fails completely
  • Control board errors — older Whirlpool and GE units sometimes need board replacement after 15+ years of service
  • Loud grinding or humming during cycle — worn wash motor bearings in ageing units; usually repairable without full motor replacement
