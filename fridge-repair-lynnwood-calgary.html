Common Fridge Repair Issues in Lynnwood

  • Not cooling — evaporator fan, refrigerant, or compressor fault; urgent same-day dispatch
  • Frost buildup in freezer — defrost heater, thermostat, or timer failure
  • Water pooling inside or on floor — blocked defrost drain or leaking water supply line
  • Ice maker not working — water inlet valve or frozen supply line
  • Clicking or buzzing noise — compressor start relay; inexpensive fix, prevents worse damage
  • Temperature inconsistency — thermistor or damper control faults
