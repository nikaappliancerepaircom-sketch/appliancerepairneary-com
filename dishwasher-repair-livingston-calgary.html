Common Dishwasher Problems in Livingston

Hard Water Scale in Circulation Pump

Calgary's 150–200 ppm mineral content accumulates in circulation pump impeller housings within the first few years of operation. Wash pressure drops, dishes come out with white film. We descale the pump housing, clean spray arms, replace inlet valve screens, and recommend a rinse aid regimen to slow future buildup.

Samsung Variable-Speed Pump Sensor Errors

Scale on flow sensors in Samsung's newer variable-speed pump systems causes false readings that reduce pump output, compounding cleaning failure. We clean sensor surfaces, flush the pump circuit, and verify restored pump speed calibration — resolving cleaning issues without a control board replacement.

LG Multi-Spray Arm Supply Restriction

LG's QuadWash and multi-arm systems have independent supply circuits. Scale or debris restriction in one circuit causes zone-specific cleaning failure. We trace the supply circuit from the restriction point back through the pump, clearing blockages to restore uniform wash performance across all rack zones.

GE Profile Control Board & WiFi Module Faults

GE's smart dishwasher WiFi connectivity modules develop faults in the 4–7-year range that can affect cycle initiation. We diagnose whether the fault is isolated to the connectivity module or the main control logic before recommending a board replacement — often the module alone is the issue.

Door Latch & Interlock Wear

High-use dishwashers in busy Livingston family homes accumulate door latch wear faster than average. When the latch fails to engage fully, the door interlock switch won't activate and cycles won't start. We replace the latch assembly and test the full interlock circuit before completing the repair.

Drain Pump & Filter Blockages

Modern dishwashers rely on self-cleaning filters, but debris that bypasses the filter can block the drain pump impeller. Standing water at the bottom of the tub is the primary symptom. We clear the drain path, inspect the pump impeller, and demonstrate correct filter maintenance to prevent recurrence.

Dishwasher Repair FAQ — Livingston

My dishwasher is only 5 years old. Is it worth repairing?

At 5 years old, a dishwasher has the majority of its service life ahead of it. Repair almost always makes economic sense for single-component failures at this age — control board modules, inlet valves, spray arms, and drain pumps are all significantly less expensive than a new appliance. We provide an upfront repair cost estimate during the diagnostic visit.

How does Calgary hard water affect a newer dishwasher?

Hard water at 150–200 ppm deposits calcium and magnesium minerals in the circulation pump, spray arms, and inlet valve screens from the first months of operation. By years 3–5, scale accumulation is typically enough to reduce wash performance noticeably. Regular rinse aid use slows buildup significantly, and we recommend professional descaling every 2–3 years for Calgary homes without a water softener.

My LG dishwasher doesn't clean the top rack properly. What causes this?

Isolated top-rack cleaning failure typically points to the upper spray arm supply circuit — either a scale restriction in the supply tower, a partially blocked upper spray arm bearing, or reduced pump pressure at the upper spray arm zone. We trace the supply path from the pump to the upper arm and clear the restriction without replacing functioning components.
