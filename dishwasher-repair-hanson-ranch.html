Hanson Ranch is one of north Calgary's newer suburban communities, developed primarily through the 2000s as part of the broader expansion north of Country Hills Boulevard. Homes here were built with builder-grade appliance packages — Samsung, LG, and GE dishwashers installed by the construction company rather than chosen by homeowners. These machines are now 8–18 years old, placing them squarely in the post-warranty, high-failure-risk window where repair is typically more economical than replacement.

Calgary's municipal water supply carries 150–200 ppm dissolved calcium and magnesium — classifying it as moderately hard water. This is the defining maintenance challenge for every dishwasher in Hanson Ranch. Over years of daily use without water softening, scale accumulates in the circulation pump impeller housing, reducing water pressure through the spray arms. Dishes come out with a white film, glasses cloud over, and eventually the pump strains under load and begins to fail. We flush circulation pumps, replace inlet valve screens, and descale internal components as part of our standard dishwasher service — addressing both the immediate fault and the underlying water quality issue.

Samsung dishwashers, which feature prominently in Hanson Ranch homes, present a recurring pattern of control board relay failure as they age. The relay that governs the heating element and wash cycle sequencing can degrade, causing the machine to stop mid-cycle or refuse to start at all. Because the symptom mirrors a door latch fault or thermal fuse failure, correct diagnosis requires testing all three before ordering a control board. We bring diagnostic equipment that confirms the board as the failure point before we commit to the repair cost — saving homeowners from unnecessary parts expense.

GE dishwashers in this community frequently develop spray arm issues. The upper spray arm, which washes dishes on the top rack, has a bearing where it connects to the water supply tower. Mineral deposits from Calgary's hard water gradually restrict this bearing, reducing spray arm rotation. The result: dishes on the top rack come out dirty even when the lower rack cleans normally. We clean or replace the spray arm bearing and flush the supply tower — a targeted repair that restores full wash performance without replacing the entire spray arm assembly when the arm itself is undamaged.

Common Dishwasher Problems in Hanson Ranch

Circulation Pump Scale Buildup

Calgary's 150–200 ppm hard water deposits scale in the circulation pump impeller housing over years of use. Water pressure through spray arms drops, dishes emerge with white film. We flush the pump, descale internal components, and replace inlet valve screens to restore full wash pressure.

Samsung Control Board Relay Failure

The relay governing heating element and cycle sequencing degrades with age, causing mid-cycle stops or refusal to start. We test the door latch, thermal fuse, and relay together to confirm the board as the fault before recommending replacement — avoiding unnecessary parts cost.

GE Upper Spray Arm Restriction

Mineral deposits restrict the upper spray arm bearing and supply tower, reducing rotation on the top rack. Dishes above the middle come out dirty while the lower rack cleans normally. We clean or replace the bearing and flush the supply tower in a targeted repair.

LG Turbidity Sensor Fouling

LG dishwashers use a turbidity sensor to measure water clarity and adjust cycle length. Calcium scale on the sensor lens causes incorrect readings — cycles run too long or stop early. We clean the sensor and test cycle calibration, restoring efficient wash performance.

Door Latch & Interlock Failure

Door latches on builder-grade dishwashers installed in the 2000s wear with daily use. When the latch fails to engage fully, the door interlock switch won't activate and the cycle won't start. We replace the latch assembly and test the interlock for secondary wear before completing the repair.

Drain Pump & Hose Blockages

Dishwashers that leave standing water at the bottom of the tub have drain pump or hose blockages. We clear debris, inspect the drain pump impeller for wear, and check the hose connection at the garbage disposal — a common blockage point in Calgary homes with InSinkErator units.

Dishwasher Repair FAQ — Hanson Ranch

Why are my dishes cloudy even after a full wash cycle?

Cloudy dishes in Calgary homes are almost always caused by mineral scale from the city's 150–200 ppm hard water. Scale deposits on dishes and glassware are difficult to remove with detergent alone. We address this by descaling the spray arms, cleaning the circulation pump, and replacing inlet valve screens — restoring wash performance and recommending a rinse aid regimen to prevent recurrence.

My Samsung dishwasher stops mid-cycle. Is this a control board problem?

Mid-cycle stops in Samsung dishwashers can be caused by the control board relay, thermal fuse, door latch, or water inlet valve. We diagnose all four before recommending a control board replacement. Many mid-cycle stops are resolved with a thermal fuse or door latch repair at lower cost than a full board swap.

Should I repair or replace a 10–12-year-old dishwasher?

At 10–12 years, most dishwashers still have meaningful service life remaining. Repair makes sense for single-component failures — pumps, valves, spray arms, door latches — where the repair cost is under 50% of replacement cost. We provide a transparent repair vs. replace analysis during the diagnostic visit so you can make an informed decision.
