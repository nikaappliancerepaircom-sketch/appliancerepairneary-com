Common Fridge Problems We Fix in Valley Ridge

The 1990s–2000s vintage appliances in Valley Ridge present a consistent set of failure patterns that our technicians recognise immediately:

  • KitchenAid ice maker ejector arm crack — plastic becomes brittle after 15+ years, causing ice jams and water pooling on the freezer floor
  • KitchenAid inlet valve solenoid failure — low water pressure from the valve stops ice production and the water dispenser simultaneously
  • Samsung Family Hub sealed system fault — the dual evaporator design requires both coils to defrost properly; a failed defrost sensor causes warm zones in either compartment
  • Bosch counter-depth condenser fan motor wear — compact condenser chambers trap lint and dust, leading to motor overheating and compressor cycling issues
  • Whirlpool French-door ice maker arm reset failure — the harvest thermostat sticks open, halting ice production without triggering an error code
  • LG linear compressor refrigerant leak — a known issue in LG units manufactured 2005–2014, requiring sealed-system evacuation and recharge

Why Valley Ridge Fridges Age Differently

Many Valley Ridge homes were built with integrated refrigerator spaces designed around specific cabinetry depths. Counter-depth and built-in fridges run hotter because airflow around the condenser is restricted by cabinetry on three sides. Combined with Calgary's wide seasonal temperature swings — from -30 °C in January to +30 °C in July — compressors and defrost systems work harder than in more temperate climates. This accelerates wear on defrost heaters, thermostats, and sealed-system components. Our technicians account for this context when diagnosing a Valley Ridge refrigerator: what looks like a simple "not cooling" complaint often traces to a compressor running at capacity with a partially blocked condenser, not an outright component failure.

My Samsung fridge is warm on one side but cold on the other — what's wrong? +
This is a classic symptom of dual-evaporator defrost failure. Samsung's two-evaporator design uses separate defrost heaters and sensors for the freezer and fresh-food compartments. When one sensor fails, only part of the fridge chills correctly. We diagnose and replace the faulty component in a single visit.

KitchenAid refrigerators are built to last 18–22 years with proper maintenance. If the compressor is still healthy and the repair cost is under 50% of replacement value, repair is usually the better choice. We'll give you an honest assessment after the diagnostic.
