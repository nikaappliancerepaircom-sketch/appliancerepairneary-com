Calgary Hard Water & Your Forest Lawn Refrigerator

In Forest Lawn's larger household configurations, refrigerators with water dispensers and ice makers face a dual stress: high demand from frequent use, and Calgary's 150–200 ppm water supply calcifying the inlet valve screen. The ice maker inlet valve may become restricted within 12–15 months without a water filter change, leading to reduced ice production or complete ice maker failure that's easy to misdiagnose as a mechanical problem. Our technicians always test water system flow rate on Forest Lawn dispenser calls, replacing calcified inlet valves and advising on the 6-month filter replacement schedule appropriate for Calgary's water hardness in high-demand households.

Tip for Forest Lawn households: In a large household with 4+ people regularly accessing the fridge, check the door gasket every 6 months by closing the door on a piece of paper — if you can pull the paper out easily, the gasket has lost its seal. A worn gasket makes the compressor run almost continuously, shortening compressor life significantly and raising your electricity bill.
