How Our Appliance Repair Service Works

Every service call follows the same efficient process: you book by phone at (437) 524-1053 or online, and we dispatch the nearest certified technician to your address with a 2-hour arrival window. On arrival, the technician diagnoses the fault and provides a written upfront quote. You approve the price before any work begins. Most repairs are completed in the same visit because our vans carry the parts most likely needed for each appliance type and brand.

Typical repair costs run $120–$180 for minor faults (switches, sensors, belts, fuses), $180–$280 for standard repairs (motors, pumps, door seals, boards), and $280–$420 for major repairs (compressors, drums, main control boards). The diagnostic visit fee of $0–$85 is waived when you proceed with the repair. All completed repairs carry a 90-day parts and labour warranty.

We service Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, Bosch, Frigidaire, GE, Kenmore, Miele, KitchenAid, and Maytag appliances. Coverage spans all of Toronto, Scarborough, North York, Etobicoke, Mississauga, Brampton, Vaughan, Richmond Hill, Markham, Oakville, Burlington, Pickering, Ajax, Whitby, and Oshawa. Hours are Monday–Saturday 8am–8pm and Sunday 9am–6pm with no overtime surcharge.

Appliance Repair Services — FAQ

Common questions about our repair services across Toronto and the GTA.

Which appliances do you repair?

We repair refrigerators, washers, dryers, dishwashers, ovens, stoves, and freezers. We handle all major brands including Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, Bosch, Frigidaire, GE, Kenmore, and Miele across the Greater Toronto Area.

Is same-day appliance repair available?

Yes. Call before noon on weekdays or Saturday for same-day service across most GTA areas. Our technicians are positioned throughout the city so the nearest tech to your address is dispatched, resulting in faster arrival times.

How much does appliance repair cost?

Typical cost ranges from $120–$420 depending on the appliance and fault. Minor repairs (sensors, fuses, belts) run $120–$180; standard repairs (pumps, motors, seals) run $180–$280; major repairs (compressors, control boards) run $280–$420. Diagnostic fee waived when you proceed with repair.

What warranty do you provide on repairs?

All repairs carry a 90-day warranty on both parts and labour. If the same fault returns within 90 days, we come back and fix it at no additional charge. The warranty is provided in writing with every completed repair.
