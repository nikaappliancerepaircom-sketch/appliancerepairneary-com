Common Dishwasher Problems in Walden Homes

  • Samsung 5E/SE drain fault — blocked filter, pump impeller obstruction, or failed drain pump motor
  • LG AE error (water leak) — door gasket failure or float switch triggered by minor leak at base of unit
  • Hard water scale on spray arms — Calgary's 150–200 ppm water producing cloudy glassware and poor wash results
  • Control board failure — cycle interruptions or dishwasher failing to start; common at 10–15 year mark
  • GE wash motor failure — weak spray pressure, dishes not cleaning; motor replaced or rebuilt
  • Water inlet valve blockage — dishwasher not filling properly; mineral deposits restricting flow

My Samsung dishwasher shows a 5E error — what does it mean?+

Samsung's 5E code (also shown as SE) indicates a drain fault — the dishwasher detected that water didn't drain properly after a cycle. Common causes include a blocked drain filter, an obstruction in the pump impeller, a kinked drain hose, or a failed drain pump motor. Many 5E errors in Walden resolve with a filter cleaning and minor blockage removal, but our technician tests the pump motor to rule out a more serious fault at the same visit.

Why are my glasses coming out cloudy in Walden?+

Cloudy glassware is almost always a hard water issue. Calgary's 150–200 ppm water hardness leaves calcium deposits on glass surfaces and inside spray arm nozzles. When the nozzles partially block, spray pressure drops and detergent doesn't fully activate — making the etching worse. We descale spray arms and flush the inlet valve during every repair visit.
