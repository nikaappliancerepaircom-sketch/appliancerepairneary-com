FAQ — Appliance Repair in Okotoks

My 20-year-old GE fridge is failing — repair or replace? +

It depends on the fault. In Okotoks' Drake Landing, Crystal Shores, and Cimarron neighborhoods, the original 2003–2008 GE GSS top-mount fridges develop defrost system faults around year 18–22 (timer, heater, or bi-metal thermostat). On a fundamentally healthy GSS, a $220 timer + heater kit gives you another 5–7 years of useful life — usually the right call. On a unit with sealed-system issues (compressor, refrigerant leak), replacement is typically the better economics. We give you the math on every call.

Should I rebuild my 17-year-old Maytag Centennial transmission? +

Almost never. The Maytag Centennial / Whirlpool direct-drive transmission seizure that we see most often in Cimarron and Suntree households runs $480–$680 to rebuild — almost the cost of a new mid-range washer with a fresh manufacturer warranty. We lay out the math: rebuild + 90-day warranty vs. new unit + 1-year manufacturer warranty + new platform reliability. On 17+ year-old top-loaders, replacement is almost always the better call.
