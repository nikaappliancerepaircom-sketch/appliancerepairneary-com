Common Maytag Dishwasher Problems We Fix

Clean Light Flashing 7 Times

Maytag heating circuit fault -- thermal fuse, heating element, or thermistor. Stocked for same-day repair.

Upper Spray Arm Not Spinning

Blocked water supply tower or cracked spray arm on Tall Tub models. Cleared or replaced same visit.

Not Draining After Cycle

Drain pump failure, blocked filter, or kinked hose. Standing water cleared and root cause fixed.

Control Board Unresponsive

Main PCB, touchpad ribbon, or display module failure. OEM-quality boards available.

Door Latch Not Closing

Latch assembly wear preventing cycle start. Fast same-day replacement.

Leaking Water

Door gasket, pump seal, or water inlet valve. Leak located and repaired on the first visit.
