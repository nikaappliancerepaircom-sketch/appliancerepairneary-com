Understanding Whirlpool Dishwasher Issues in GTA Homes

Whirlpool dishwashers are the most common appliance we service in GTA West homes. The clean light blinking 7 times signals a heating circuit fault -- thermal fuse failure is most common, often triggered by a power surge. Door latch failure is the number-one cause of a Whirlpool that will not start -- the latch contacts a micro-switch that signals the control board. When the latch wears, the cycle never initiates. Drain pump issues are usually preceded by a clogged filter -- monthly cleaning prevents most drain failures. We carry all common Whirlpool platform components in stock.
