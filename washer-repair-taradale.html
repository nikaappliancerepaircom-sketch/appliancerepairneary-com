Common Washer Issues in Taradale Homes

  • Drain pump failure — heavy laundry loads in large Taradale households accelerate pump impeller wear and lint blockage

  • Door boot gasket leaking — cracked door seals in front-load washers; leads to water on floor if not addressed
  • Not spinning / UE error — suspension rod failure or load imbalance; often linked to overloading in large-household use patterns
  • Control board errors (4E, 5E, dE) — Samsung and LG electronics past warranty showing intermittent and persistent faults
  • Detergent drawer mould / dispenser blockage — Calgary hard water deposits and residual detergent combine to block the dispenser channel
